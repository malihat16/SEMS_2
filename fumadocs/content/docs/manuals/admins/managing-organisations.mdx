### Accessing Organisation Management

Go to the SEMS Organisation Management Page under the Admins tab in the navigation menu.

![SEMS Organisation Management](/images/SEMS_Admin_Managing_Organisations.png)

### What You Can Do

Admins can manage organisation details, memberships, and permissions across the platform.  
For more detailed steps on managing specific organisation aspects, refer to the [Managing Organisations](/docs/manuals/organisers/managing-organisations) guide.
