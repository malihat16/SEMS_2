Analytics and reporting on SEMS provide organisers with insights into event performance, participant engagement, and overall organisational metrics.

<Callout title="Prerequisites">
    You must be a SEMS administrator to access analytics and reporting features on SEMS.  
    Ensure you have the necessary permissions before proceeding.
</Callout>

### Accessing Analytics & Reporting

Go to the SEMS Analytics & Reporting Page under the Admins tab in the navigation menu.

![SEMS Analytics & Reporting](/images/SEMS_Analytics_Reporting.png)

### What You Can Do

- View detailed reports on event attendance, participant demographics, and engagement metrics.
- Export data for further analysis or record-keeping. (CSV and Excel)
- Customize, generate, and download reports based on specific date ranges. (PDF)
