As an organiser, managing your organisation on SEMS involves overseeing various aspects such as members, events, and settings.

<Callout title="Prerequisites">
    You must be an organisation owner or leader to manage organisations on SEMS.  
    Ensure you have the necessary permissions before proceeding.
</Callout>

### Accessing Organisation Management

Go to the SEMS Organisation Management Page under the Organisers tab in the navigation menu.

![SEMS Organisation Management 1](/images/SEMS_Organisation_Management_1.png)

### Organisation Members Page for Organisers

Under the organisation members page, you can manage various aspects of your organisation such as adding or removing members, assigning roles, and viewing member details.

<Callout title="Prerequisites">
    You must be an organisation owner to manage organisation member roles on SEMS.  
    You must be an organisation owner or leader to manage organisation members on SEMS.  
    Ensure you have the necessary permissions before proceeding.
</Callout>

![SEMS Organisation Management 2](/images/SEMS_Organisation_Management_2.png)

### Managing Organisation Details

Update organisation details including organisation name, description, and other relevant information by clicking the "Edit Organisation" button.

<Callout title="Prerequisites">
    You must be an organisation owner to manage organisation details on SEMS.  
    Ensure you have the necessary permissions before proceeding.
</Callout>

![SEMS Organisation Management 3](/images/SEMS_Organisation_Management_3.png)
