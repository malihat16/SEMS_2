## Where do I find my Upcoming Events?

To keep track of your upcoming registered events, navigate to the "My Upcoming" section on the SEMS Platform Event Page. This section provides a comprehensive list of all the events you have registered for, along with their details.

![SEMS Events 5](/images/SEMS_Events_5.png)

## What's Important?

Event details such as "Note to Registrants" and "Check-in QR Code" are accessible after registration.  
Keep an eye on these details for any important information regarding the event.  
Prepare the Check-in QR code for check-in on the event day.

<Callout title="Note">
    You can also find the Check-in QR code and other event details in the event registration confirmation email sent to you.  
    A reminder email may also be sent closer to the event date, which will include these details as well.  
</Callout>
