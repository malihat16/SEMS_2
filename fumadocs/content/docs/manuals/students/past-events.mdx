## Where do I find my Past Events?

To keep track of your registered events history, navigate to the "My Past" section on the SEMS Platform Event Page. This section provides a comprehensive list of all the events you have registered for, along with their details.

![SEMS Events 6](/images/SEMS_Events_6.png)

## What's Next?

After attending an event, if the organiser has requested feedback, you can provide it for the event in the "My Past" section. Use this section to review your event history and reflect on your participation.

![SEMS Events 7](/images/SEMS_Events_7.png)
