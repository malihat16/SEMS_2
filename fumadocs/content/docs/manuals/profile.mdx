Your profile on the SEMS Platform contains important information that helps organisers manage your participation in events. Keeping your profile up-to-date ensures a smooth experience when joining and attending events.

## Accessing Your Profile

To access your profile, click on your avatar or initials located at the top-right corner of the SEMS Platform, then select "Profile" from the dropdown menu.

## Editing Your Profile

To edit your profile information, update the necessary fields and click "Request Update" to save your changes. Note that some fields may require verification by a SEMS administrator before the changes take effect.
